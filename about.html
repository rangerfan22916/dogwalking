About Drew

Hi! I'm Drew, a passionate and dedicated dog walker who treats every pup like family. With several years of experience and a genuine love for animals, I’m committed to making sure your dog is happy, healthy, and safe every time we’re together.

Whether your dog needs a quick potty break, an energetic walk, or a fun-filled play session, I show up with enthusiasm, reliability, and care. I’ve worked with dogs of all breeds, sizes, and personalities — from energetic puppies to senior companions — and I take the time to get to know each dog’s unique needs and routines.

I currently serve multiple neighborhoods and offer flexible scheduling to fit your lifestyle. Whether you're at work, away on vacation, or just need a helping hand, you can count on me to give your furry friend the attention and love they deserve.
